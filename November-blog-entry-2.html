Entry 2 for November

Eoghan Canty Sat 27/11, 2021

For the second entry of the month I have a number of things to update on. As explained in my previous entry of this month, I was about to start my design presentation. This presentation was essentially developing everything graphically to do with my brand. I am developing multiple assets to for this presentation. These include: logo, moodboard, website navigation, personas, wireframes, interface designs and the final design presentation itself. The first element I designed was the logo. For every brand a logo is essential. There should be a lot of meaning behind. For my logo as I was targeting the Irish market I wanted this to have some connection to Irish people. As the name of my product is The F1 Guide I wanted to have some meaning to the logo indicating it is an educational brand. In then end I went with a figure of eight element sitting in the middle with the words “The” and “F1” either side of it. Below the figure of eight I have the word “Guide”. The figure of eight is coloured green white and gold which is obviously the colours of the Irish flag. This is the meaning I was looking for. The figure of eight also has some meaning behind it. This element traditionally represents a racetrack icon which is obviously synonymous with Formula 1 racing. This element also signifies another message. You start at the beginning of the lap in which you are lacking a lot of knowledge of F1 but by the end you finish the lap and you should be an experienced and knowledgeable F1 fan. I was quite happy with this logo but would have liked more time to define it. The second asset I designed was a moodboard. For this I researched elements that I would be using on my website and AR app. These elements include colours, fonts, layouts, themes and 3D models. From doing this moodboard I’ve now been able to understand the overall look of how I want my website and AR app to look especially in the sense of colours, fonts and layout. The next asset I wanted to deliver was my website navigation. For this I had to identify the sections of my website. I want to have a video section and article section. I want to have a download page for my AR app. I decided for my navigation that I can combine the videos and articles section into one navigation element called “understanding F1”. This will ensure the navigation bar is not clustered with elements. Below the “understanding F1” element on the website it will go down a level to the video or article section which are on the same level. Below these levels a specific article or video page will be there. This will be the final level. As you can see the navigation is not too deep. The final asset I have done up to now is a persona. The personas James and Amy represent everything that I am trying to build for them. Eliminating a poor user experience and providing exciting consumable content is the goal.

Overall, up to now I am quite pleased to what I have got done and over the next weeks I am planning to finish off the rest of the assets and the presentation ahead the deadline.
