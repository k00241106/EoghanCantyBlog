Entry 1 for November

Eoghan Canty Wed 13/11, 2021

Welcome to my blog for November! After October I found it a very difficult and straining month both mentally and physically. I had a lot of work to do for my project this month. According to my gantt chart, I was going to be tackling the design presentation this month. This part of the project would mean I would be essentially building out my brand that I had proposed in September’s blog. Involved in the design presentation I will be delivering a number of elements. The first element I will design is my brand logo. As reported in my previous blogs, I am developing an educational brand. The second element I am planning to deliver is a moodboard. This will give me an indication of colours, fonts and overall theme I want to apply to both my website and AR application. The third element I want to deliver is my website’s navigation. Designing a navigation will allow me to understand the depth of my website. A website that goes down too many levels will likely lead to a poor user experience. After developing these elements I want to build out a persona. Despite designing one in the last report I wanted to design one more to gain an even better understanding of my ideal user with the design elements listed. From the knowledge I gained I will then design my wireframes. These wireframes will be simple black and white drawings done in Photoshop. I will develop the wireframes for both my website and my AR app. With these wireframes designed I will then be able to build my interface design. This will essentially be a high-fidelity prototype with all the expected colours, fonts and layout. Just like the wireframes I will build the interface designs for both my website and AR app. The final task I will complete for November will be my design presentation. This presentation will involve showing off most of the elements listed above in front of the FYP supervisors.

Concluding this entry I am quite nervous about the month ahead as there is quite a lot of work involved here. Designing these elements takes a lot of love and care to do and with other assignments which are not FYP related thrown in the mix as well this will be quite tough.
