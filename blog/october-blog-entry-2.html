Entry 2 for October

Eoghan Canty Fri 22/10, 2021

Once I finished the gantt chart I knew what tasks I needed to do every month to achieve my goal of building and completing a high standard final year project (FYP). This month I needed to move onto the research report. This was a report in which I had to investigate a number of factors that related to my project. The first section I looked at were the benefits to building my particular project. To summarise the benefits I clearly mentioned the advantages for making this project were because of the positive impact sport brings to people. I read an article from First Touch Online that explained how important sport is. The benefits included: social improvement, better self-esteem, more stable mood and finally it’s entertainment. I then moved onto the target market. For this section I had to make a survey and send it out to the appropriate audience I wanted to ask questions to. I made the survey using Survey Monkey. I asked 10 questions that related to physical questions such as age and gender but also more personal questions such as their experience when it came to watching Formula 1. I used popular social media platform Discord to ask my questions to related F1 groups. I ended up getting 20 replies. Once I got my data I tried to understand it. Once writing about my target audience I was able to generate three personas. These all related to my target audience research. Following the target audience section I then researched competitors who had similar products to the one I was proposing. This was a particularly long section as I had to ensure I had at least two competitors for both my website and AR app. For my website competitors I found Tutorials Point and F1 Chronicle. For my AR app I found another two competitors. These were AR Real Driving and OculAR. As of now I currently have up to this section done. After the competitors section I am now moving onto the Technologies section. In this section I will talk about all the different technologies I want to use such as my web development stack and API’s. I will then conclude the report with some quick but important sections such as design concepts, search engine optimisation, deliverables and my eventual conclusion.
